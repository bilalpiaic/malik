Knitting Machine

A knitting machine is a device used to automate the process of knitting, creating fabrics from yarn more efficiently and consistently than manual hand-knitting. It consists of multiple needles arranged on a needle bed, and these needles interact with the yarn to produce knitted fabrics of various textures and designs. Knitting machines can produce flat or tubular fabrics and are widely used in the textile industry to create items like sweaters, socks, and other knitwear. They come in various types, each catering to different kinds of fabrics and designs.

Key Features:

  • Production Line
  • High-efficiency motor
  • Polister/Cotton Flees
